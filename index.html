About Oxlo

At Oxlo, we’re building a smart, student-focused chatbot designed to support college students in their everyday academic journey. Whether you're facing challenges with your University Management System (UMS), struggling with coursework, or simply need guidance—Oxlo is here to help.

Our chatbot delivers well-structured lectures curated from top open-source educators, making quality learning accessible and personalized.

To bring this vision to life, we’ve assembled a passionate team of 30+ students and professionals who are contributing their skills to create a platform that truly empowers learners.

Together, we’re making academic support smarter, faster, and more human.

We also offer open source resources for studies and projects.
